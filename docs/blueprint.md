# **App Name**: GlycoAI

## Core Features:

- Biodata Recording: Record and store patient biodata: height, weight, age, insulin brand, etc.
- Insulin Dosage Tracking: Record and track insulin units consumed.
- AI Glucose Prediction: AI-powered blood glucose prediction based on user data and glucose-insulin models. The AI will make use of an advanced inference tool that provides the reasoning for all predictions and behaviors shown.
- Personalized Tips and Suggestions: Provide tips on maintaining blood glucose levels naturally, tailored to the user's data.
- Explanation of Unusual Behaviors: Explain potential causes of unusual blood glucose behaviors, such as spikes after exercise, and include reasons.
- Solutions for Common Issues: Offer solutions for issues like dawn phenomenon and nighttime hypoglycemia.
- Multi-Language Support: Provide support for multiple languages to ensure accessibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A7D1E8) to evoke calmness and health. The hue is a gentle sky-blue, which avoids the cliches associated with medical apps.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF), almost white, providing a clean and clinical feel.
- Accent color: Pale orange (#FFB347) for interactive elements and important notifications. This hue is closely analogous to the blue.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth.
- Use clear, easily understandable icons that are universally recognizable, aiding usability for all ages.
- Intuitive layout with clear visual hierarchy to ensure ease of use, particularly for elderly and young users.
- Subtle transitions and animations to guide users and provide feedback without overwhelming them.
